What is Nier:Automata ?

Nier:Automata is a 2017 action role-playing game developed by PlatinumGames and published by SquareEnix in which players take the role of combat androids from the YoRHa units across a big open world*.

As characters progress, they gain experience levels through combat which is hack and slash action-based, with the player fighting enemies in real-time and various in-game environments.

Some fights are easy and others can be really hard, if you are a hot-blooded person you will quickly run out of patience and be angry, trust me I know what I'm talking about. Throughout the game they can increase their health, defence, and attack power. To do that players must gain experience and accomplishing quests can help you to do that.

The game shares the post-apocalyptic setting of Nier, taking place thousands of years after the game's fourth ending. The universe of Nier takes place in an alternate timeline within the Drakengard series. While carrying over the Drakengard tradition of a dark atmosphere and amazing storylines, there is no narrative connection between Nier: Automata and the rest of the series.

* an open world means that you can explore everything and everywhere you want.
