What is Nier:Automata ?

Nier: Automata is a 2017 action role-playing game developed by PlatinumGames and published by SquareEnix in which players take the role of combat androids from the YoRHa units across an open world.
As characters progress, they gain experience levels through combat which is hack and slash action-based, with the player fighting enemies in real-time and various in-game environments. Throughout the game players can increase their health, defence, and attack power.
The game shares the post-apocalyptic setting of Nier, taking place thousands of years after the game's fourth ending.
The universe of Nier takes place in an alternate timeline within the Drakengard series. While carrying over the Drakengard tradition of a dark atmosphere and amazing storylines, no direct narrative connection is shared between Nier: Automata and the rest of the series.
